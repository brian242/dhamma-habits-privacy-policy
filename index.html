Privacy Policy for Dhamma Habits

Effective date: [insert date]

Dhamma Habits is developed by Brian Naik.

Dhamma Habits is a Buddhist practice habit-tracking app. It lets users browse practice habits, add habits to My Practice, record practice logs, save personal notes, set optional practice goals, view glossary terms and references, and export or import backups.

Data stored on your device

Dhamma Habits stores selected habits, practice logs, practice goals, personal notes, app settings, onboarding status, and backup files you choose to export.

This data is stored locally on your device. The app does not send this practice data to the developer.

Backups

The app lets you export and import backup files. Backup files can contain your selected habits, logs, goals, notes, and settings. You are responsible for where you save, store, or share exported backup files.

External links

Dhamma Habits can open external websites when you tap reference links or the voluntary donation link. These websites are outside the app and have their own privacy practices.

Donations

The app includes a voluntary donation link. Donations are handled by an external payment provider. Dhamma Habits does not directly process or store payment card details.

Data sharing

Dhamma Habits does not sell user data. The app does not intentionally share your practice data with the developer or third parties.

Data security

Your app data is stored locally on your device. You should protect your device and exported backup files, especially if your personal notes contain private information.

Data deletion

You can delete your practice data by using the reset option in the app or by uninstalling the app. Exported backup files must be deleted separately from wherever you saved them.

Children

Dhamma Habits is not specifically directed at children.
